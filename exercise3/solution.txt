User scenarios:
1. Roll a die and move: As a player, when it is my turn I can roll a die so that my token moves forward on the board by the number on the die
2. Buy free property: As a player, when my token lands on free property I can buy it so that I can collect rent from other players
3. Pay rent: As a player, I can automatically pay rent to other players when my token lands on their property
4. Go to jail: As a player, I can be sent directly to jail when my token lands on the "go to jail" field, which ends my turn immediately and makes me skip 3 turns

Acceptance tests:
1.
GIVEN it is player's turn and their token is on the "go" field
WHEN the player rolls a die and gets 3
THEN the player's token moves forward 3 spaces

2. 
GIVEN player's token lands on free property and it costs 400$
AND the player has a balance of 1500$
WHEN the player chooses to buy the property
THEN the player should be registered as the owner of the property
AND player's balance decreases to 1100$

3.
GIVEN player 1's token lands on a "mansion X" field, which currently has 35$ rent
AND player 2 owns "mansion X"
WHEN player 1's token lands on "mansion X"
THEN player 1's balance decreases by 35$
AND player 2's balance increases by 35$

4.
GIVEN player's token is on a field 29
AND player's token moves forward by 1 spaces
WHEN player's token lands on "go to jail" field (field 30)
THEN player's token is moved to the "in jail" field and player's turn immediately ends
